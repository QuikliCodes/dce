# Great Leap (I - III)

**Cost: 1 per feat**

## Description

Through extraordinary power or exceptional agility, you can jump much
farther than the average creature. A telekinetic superhero and an
insectoid race of bipedal cricket-like beings are both examples of this
feat in play.

## Effect

You can use your [Speed](Speed.md) score instead of your [Power](Power.md) score
when determining how far you can jump. In addition, you gain advantage 1
per tier on action rolls to jump.
