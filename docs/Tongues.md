# Tongues

## Properties

-   Min. Power Level: 5
-   Invocation Time: 10 Minutes
-   Duration: Sustain Persists

## Description

You or an ally temporarily gains the ability to read, write, and speak
languages that they are otherwise unfamiliar with. This ability may come
by channeling interplanar spirits, tapping the power of a super
intelligence, making deductions based on an extraordinary understanding
of the science of communication, or similar means.

## Effect

-   **Power Level 5** - Your target can understand and speak a language
    of your choice.
-   **Power Level 6** - Your target can read a language of your choice.
