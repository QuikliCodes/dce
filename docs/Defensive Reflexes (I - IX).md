# Defensive Reflexes (I - IX)

**Cost: 2 per tier**

## Description

You are a paragon of defensive fighting, able to masterfully thwart
enemy attacks. By predicting your opponents' movements, strikes, or
shots, you can cut them off before they are a threat.

## Effect

Any time you use the defend action, you gain advantage 1 on the action
roll per tier you possess of this feat.
