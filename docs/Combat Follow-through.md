# Combat Follow-through

**Cost: 2**

## Description

You are able to decimate many enemies in quick succession. Like a
legendary Samurai warrior or matchless elven archer, your foes fall in
waves before you.

## Effect

When you bring an enemy to zero hit points with a
[Power](Power.md) or [Speed](Speed.md) attack, you can immediately make an
extra attack as a minor action.
