# Weapons

## Weapon Categories & Effects

- **One-Handed Melee:** Wielding a one-handed weapon in each hand (neither with the `defensive` property) grants **[advantage](Advantage.md) 1** on all melee attacks.
- **Two-Handed Melee:** Requires two hands and grants **[advantage](Advantage.md) 1** on all attacks.
- **Versatile Melee:** Can be used one-handed or two-handed, gaining the benefits of the current mode.
- **Ranged:** Can attack up to their range increment without penalty.
    - Attacks up to 2x range increment suffer **[disadvantage](Advantage.md) 1**.
    - Attacks up to 3x range increment suffer **[disadvantage](Advantage.md) 2**.
    - Attacks beyond 3x range are not possible.

## Key Weapon Properties

- **Area:** The weapon makes area attacks of a set size and does not incur [multi-targeting](Turn Actions.md#multi-targeting) penalties.
- **Defensive (X):** Grants [advantage](Advantage.md) X on [defend actions](Turn Actions.md#interrupt-actions) and a +1 armor bonus to Guard. Negates the standard [advantage](Advantage.md) from one-handed/two-handed fighting styles.
- **Delayed Ready:** Can only be used once per round and requires a [move action](Turn Actions.md#move-actions) to ready beforehand.
- **Forceful:** Can use the [Power](Power.md) attribute for attacks.
- **Precise:** Can use the [Speed](Speed.md) attribute for attacks.
- **Reach:** Extends melee range by 1 hex.
- **Slow:** [Disadvantage](Advantage.md) 2 on [initiative](Initiative.md) rolls.
- **Swift:** [Advantage](Advantage.md) 2 on [initiative](Initiative.md) rolls.

## Weapon Banes

Weapons can list specific banes they excel at inflicting.

- You can choose to try to inflict the [bane](Banes.md) instead of dealing damage
- The required attribute score for invoking the [bane](Banes.md) is treated as one lower.
- You gain **advantage 1** on the bane [attack roll](Turn Actions.md#major-actions).
