# Tough as Nails (I - II)

**Cost: 2 per tier**

## Description

You have a remarkable ability to shrug off pain and punishment that
would take down lesser heroes. This power might stem from your rocky
carapace, extreme combat training, or simply an extra dose of grit that
you acquired from a hard life.

## Effect

You permanently gain 5 extra hit points per tier of this feat you
possess.
