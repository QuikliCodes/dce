# Armor

- Armor provides a **[Defense](Defenses.md) Bonus** to your Guard score.
- It may have a **Required [Recovery](Recovery.md)** score to wear effectively.
- It may apply a **[Speed](Speed.md) Penalty**.
- **Donning/Doffing:** Takes 1 round (light), 1 minute (medium), or 10 minutes (heavy).
- **Sleeping in Armor:** Sleeping in medium or heavy armor without the Armor Mastery feat inflicts one level of the [Fatigued](Fatigued.md) bane.
