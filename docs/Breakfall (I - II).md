# Breakfall (I - II)

**Cost: 2 per tier**

## Description

You are as graceful as a swan. Like the martial artists and
swashbucklers of legendary tales, your agility enables you to fall from
deadly heights unharmed.

## Effect

-   **Tier 1** - As long as you are conscious and able to act, reduce
    all falling damage by half.
-   **Tier 2** - As long as you are conscious and able to act, you do
    not suffer falling damage.
