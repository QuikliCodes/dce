# Deafened

## Properties

-   Min. Power Level: 4
-   Invocation Time: 1 Major Action
-   Duration: Resist ends (Fail x 3 = 1 minute)

## Attributes

-   Speed
-   Spirit

## Description

You deafen your foe with a crash of thunder, a deft strike to their
ears, or a dark energy that disables their hearing. This bane is common
to storm mages, mad scientists, and assassins.

## Effect

The target cannot hear as long as the effect persists. The target
automatically fails any [Spirit](Spirit.md) rolls based solely on hearing.
Perception rolls based partially on hearing that can be supplemented by
another sense suffer disadvantage 3.
