# Defenses

Defenses are used to protect a character against attacks and banes.  When an
attack is taken, the attacker will make an [Action Roll](Action Roll.md) against a [CR](CR.md) determined
by the appropriate defense to protect against the nature of the attack or bane.


## Guard Defense

**Calculation:** 10 + Speed + Power + Armor

Represents the character's ability to dodge, overpower, deflect, or block an
attack. Used as defense against attacks or banes that can be avoided or
deflected.


## Toughness Defense

**Calculation:** 10 + Power + Recovery

Represents the character's ability to absorb or endure an attack.  Used as
defense against attacks or banes that have physical impacts which cannot be
avoided or deflected.


## Resolve Defense

**Calculation:** 10 + Spirit + Recovery

Represents the character's mental and magical resolve and endurance.  Used as
defense against attacks or banes that target the mind or magical matrix of the
body.
