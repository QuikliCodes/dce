## Legend Points

Players can spend Legend Points to improve their chances on critical rolls.

### Earning Legend Points

- Characters start with 0 and can have a maximum of 10.
- Defeating enemies higher rank than yourself results in production of legend points.  The party decides how the points are divided among the characters.

### Spending Legend Points

- A character can spend a Legend Points to empower an action.
- Points must be declared **before** making the action roll.
- For each Legend Point spent, the character gains:
    - **Advantage 1** on the roll.
    - **A +1 bonus** to the final result of the roll.
