# Lightning Reflexes (I - V)

**Cost: 1 per tier**

## Description

You are always ready for danger, allowing you to easily get the drop on
your foes. A highly trained gunslinger with a vigilant trigger finger
and a covert operative always prepared for ambush are good examples of
this feat in action.

## Effect

For each tier you possess in this feat, you gain advantage 1 on all
initiative rolls.
