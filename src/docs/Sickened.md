# Sickened

## Properties

-   Min. Power Level: 5
-   Invocation Time: 1 Major Action
-   Duration: Resist ends (Fail x 3 = 1 minute)

## Attributes

-   Spirit

## Description

Entropic energy overcomes the target, bombarding their system and
inducing nausea that makes self-defense and any kind of action
difficult. This bane might stem from a witch's curse, chemical warfare,
a powerful poison, or exposure to alien toxins.

## Effect

The target has disadvantage 1 on all action rolls and -1 to all
defenses.
