# Flight

## Properties

-   Min. Power Level: 5
-   Invocation Time: 1 Major Action
-   Duration: Sustain Persists

## Description

By sprouting wings, activating an anti-gravity device, focusing
telekinetic power, or similar means, the target takes to the skies.

## Effect

-   **Power Level 5** - The target gains a flight speed of 2 hexes.
-   **Power Level 6** - The target gains a flight speed of 12 hexes.
-   **Power Level 8** - The target gains a flight speed of 24 hexes.

If the boon is dispelled while the target is still in flight, they
plummet to the ground immediately.
