# Resilient

**Cost: 3**

## Description

Whether through luck, extraordinary will, or exceptional courage, you
are able to shake off banes quicker than others. This feat can fit
almost any character concept: a halfling who always has luck shining on
him, a sorceress with an aura of protection, a paladin blessed by a
deity, or an absent-minded professor who has no time to let disturbances
get in the way of study.

## Effect

Any time you make a resist roll, you have advantage 1 on the roll.
