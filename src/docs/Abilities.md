# Abilities

Abilities are gained when absorbing essences or using awakening stones.

An ability unlocks Banes, Boons, Special Attacks, or ways of using those which
are already unlocked. Each ability has a name and a description.  Specifics
about [Banes](Banes.md), [Boons](Boons.md), and [Special Attacks](Special Attacks.md)
can be found on their respective pages.

## Example Abilities


### Bane Attack

**Throw Sand**

You reach into your sand pouch and throw sand into a cone in front of you,
causing those within the area effect that rely on eyes to see to be attacked
with the [Blinded](Blinded.md) bane.


### Special Attack

**Hamstring Slice**

You slice at your target's legs, wings, or other physical appendages the
creature relies on for movement, pinpointing vital ligaments.  Make a regular
attack, when you succeed by more than 5, you automatically inflict the
[Slowed](Slowed.md) bane.
