# Boons

## Boon Index

- **[Absorb Object](Absorb Object.md):** Hide an object from the perception of others.
- **[Animation](Animation.md):** Create an autonomous being from inanimate matter
- **[Aura](Aura.md):** Radiate a bane or boon out to a radius around you.
- **[Barrier](Barrier.md):** Create an area of effect (Damaging, Obscuring, Hindering, Impassable, Baneful)
- **[Blindsight](Blindsight.md):** Immune to blinded bane and able to see in darkness
- **[Bolster](Bolster.md):** Advantage on action rolls with specific attribute
- **[Concealment](Concealment.md):** TODO
- **[Darkness](Darkness.md):** Create an area of darkness around a point or object
- **[Detection](Detection.md):** Detect the influence of magical auras such as life, death, holy, etc.
- **[Flight](Flight.md):** Target gains a flying speed that increases with rank.
- **[Genesis](Genesis.md):** Create matter, of varying levels of permanence and quality based on rank.
- **[Haste](Haste.md):** Increases movement speed and guard, extra actions at high rank.
- **[Heal](Heal.md):** Restores non-lethal damage
- **[Insubstantial](Insubstantial.md):** Become ethereal, able to pass through solid objects as if they were unoccupied.
- **[Invisible](Invisible.md):** Advantage to hide, increased guard
- **[Life Drain](Life Drain.md):** Heal half of damage done while boon is sustained
- **[Light](Light.md):** Create an area of light around a point or object
- **[Precognition](Precognition.md):** Have visions or insights into near-future events
- **[Reading](Reading.md):** Identify information about an object or location
- **[Regeneration](Regeneration.md):** Heal non-lethal damage over time
- **[Resistance](Resistance.md):** Increases defenses against a type of attack
- **[Restoration](Restoration.md):** Cancel out banes affecting the target
- **[Seeing](Seeing.md):** See through the eyes of a willing ally
- **[Shapeshift](Shapeshift.md):** Transform a target into another creature
- **[Summon Creature](Summon Creature.md):** Create temporary summoned minions
- **[Sustenance](Sustenance.md):** Protect against an environmental danger, biological need, or similar condition
- **[Telekinesis](Telekinesis.md):** Magically move objects
- **[Telepathy](Telepathy.md):** Communicate through thought
- **[Teleport](Teleport.md):** Instantaneously move to a target within range
- **[Tongues](Tongues.md):** Target learns a language for the duration
- **[Transmutation](Transmutation.md):** Transform matter into another object
- **[Truesight](Truesight.md):** See the presence of extraordinary effects
