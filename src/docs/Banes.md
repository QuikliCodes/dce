# Banes

## Index

- **[Blinded](Blinded.md):** The target can't see, impacting guard defense, perception, and attacks.
- **[Charmed](Charmed.md):** Change a target's attitude or make the target a friend or lover.
- **[Deafened](Deafened.md):** The target can't hear, impacting perception.
- **[Death](Death.md):** The target is rendered immobile, unconscious, and unable to take actions. 3 failed resists = death.
- **[Demoralized](Demoralized.md):** The target has disadvantage on all action rolls.
- **[Disarmed](Disarmed.md):** The target drops their held object or you take it.
- **[Dominated](Dominated.md):** The target is mind controlled to various extents depending on rank.
- **[Fatigued](Fatigued.md):** A stacking bane that scales in power, ranging from disadvantage on abilities, loss of movement speed, and eventually loss of consciousness and death.
- **[Fear](Fear.md):** Force the target to move as far away from you as it can on its turn.
- **[Forced Move](Forced Move.md):** The target is moved against their will.
- **[Immobile](Immobile.md):** the target cannot move, if melee this is a grapple.
- **[Incapacitated](Incapacitated.md):** Implies Immobile.  Unconscious. Difficulty to break scales with rank.
- **[Knockdown](Knockdown.md):** The target falls prone.  Impacts guard and target's attacks have disadvantage.
- **[Memory Alteration](Memory Alteration.md):** The target has memories replaced.
- **[Mind Dredge](Mind Dredge.md):** Spy on the thoughts of the target.
- **[Nullify](Nullify.md):** Cancel out a boon on the target
- **[Persistent Damage](Persistent Damage.md):** Target takes damage every round of combat
- **[Phantasm](Phantasm.md):** Creates an illusion of senses, depending on rank
- **[Polymorph](Polymorph.md):** Transform the target into another creature.  Rank determines how different the creature can be.
- **[Provoked](Provoked.md):** Taunt the target, giving it disadvantage on attacks against people other than you.
- **[Sickened](Sickened.md):** Target has disadvantage on action rolls and reduced defenses.
- **[Silenced](Silenced.md):** Target cannot make noise.
- **[Slowed](Slowed.md):** Target has movement speed halved.
- **[Spying](Spying.md):** Remotely observe a person or area you are familiar with.
- **[Stunned](Stunned.md):** Reduce target to a single major, single move, or single minor action
- **[Stupefied](Stupefied.md):** Produce sever brain fog in the target
- **[Truthfulness](Truthfulness.md):** The target answers questions to the best of their abilities.
